FAQ

Common buyer questions

The FAQ cards give your storefront a more complete marketplace feel and answer the questions most shoppers ask first.

Can buyers switch between monthly and yearly plans?

Yes. The toggle changes pricing on the cards instantly so visitors can compare billing options fast.

Does the cart update live?

Yes. Adding or removing items updates subtotal, fee, discount, and total in real time.

Can this turn into a real store later?

Yes. A developer can connect this front-end demo to a database, payment gateway, and order system.

Will it work on phones?

Yes. The layout stacks cleanly on mobile and keeps buttons large enough for touch use.
